The Philosophy

Lisettamare:
A Sanctuary 10 Meters from the Sea

The house is situated in Cetraro Marina, baptized "Lisettamare" for its profound connection to the water. It is a space defined by white cream tones, natural wood, and cool limewashed floors.

It enjoys a spectacular panorama for relaxing, listening to the voice of the sea, immersing oneself in clear turquoise waters, and meditating at the sight of fiery red sunsets.
